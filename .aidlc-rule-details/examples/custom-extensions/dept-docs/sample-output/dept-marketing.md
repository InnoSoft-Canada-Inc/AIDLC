## Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Multi-Channel Retailer
**Persona**: E-commerce Operations Manager
**Problem**: Overselling causes customer complaints and refund costs when manually updating inventory across Shopify, Amazon, and eBay
**Solution**: Real-time sync automatically updates stock across all channels the moment a sale happens
**Outcome**: 95% reduction in overselling incidents, 10 hours/week saved on manual updates

### Use Case 2: Growing Business Expanding to New Channels
**Persona**: Small Business Owner
**Problem**: Wants to expand from Shopify to Amazon but fears inventory chaos from managing two platforms
**Solution**: Add Amazon channel in one click, inventory syncs automatically with zero manual work
**Outcome**: Confidently expand to new sales channels without hiring additional staff

### Use Case 3: High-Volume Seller
**Persona**: Wholesale Distributor
**Problem**: Flash sales on one channel cause overselling on other channels due to batch sync delays
**Solution**: Real-time sync updates inventory instantly, even during high-volume sales events
**Outcome**: Run flash sales without inventory chaos, increase conversion by 8%
